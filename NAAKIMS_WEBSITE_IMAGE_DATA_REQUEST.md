## 12. FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

### Q: What if we don't have professional photos?
**A:** Either from the last event, ensure good lighting and clear visibility of subjects. High-quality smartphone photos are acceptable if well-composed.

### Q: Can we submit photos gradually?
**A:** Yes, but priority items should be submitted by Phase 1 deadline. Ongoing additions can be made, with website updates rolling out accordingly.

### Q: What about privacy/consent?
**A:** All individuals in photos must consent to website publication. Include signed consent forms or verbal confirmation from chapter leads.

### Q: Who will credit photographers?
**A:** Yes, photographer names will be noted in image metadata. Please provide photographer information with each submission.

### Q: Can we update information later?
**A:** Yes, updates can be submitted anytime. A revision form will be available for corrections and additions.
